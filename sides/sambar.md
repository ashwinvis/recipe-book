# Sambar

Spicy curry typically served with parboild rice.

## Things you need

Main ingredients
- 1 cup of lentils
- 1 Tomato
- 2 Potatoes
- 1 drum sticks
- 250 g okra

For the spice mix
- 1 tsp chilly powder
- 2 tsp coriander powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 pinch of asafoetida

## Instructions

- Boil all the main ingredients
- Saute the spice mix
- Mix it together
